Community Storytelling

Echoes from the River: Stories of Jammu

Join our session focused on capturing and documenting community narratives, oral histories, and personal memories associated with Jammu's landmarks and the River Tawi. This session emphasizes the intangible cultural heritage that lies within the voices of its people.

Selected stories and interviews will be digitally archived and featured in our forthcoming documentary series, ensuring your family's history becomes part of the city's documented legacy.
